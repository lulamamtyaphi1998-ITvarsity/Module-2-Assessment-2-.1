PEP

PEP is a South African institution that started in 1965 with one store. Today PEP is the largest single brand retailer in the country with stores in almost every town and village, where it often provides a lifeline to local people with affordable, good quality merchandise. PEP’s essentials include clothing and footwear, cellular products and airtime, home ware, FMCG products as well financial products and services such as funeral cove, e-wallet, account payments Top T.V and loans. Over half of all PEP’s store managers are promoted from within the company.
