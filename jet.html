Jet

If you are looking for trendy, classy, and fun clothes for the whole family then Jet Stores is for you. Jet offers stylish, fashionable clothes within a family friendly budget. Jet was launched in November 2011 to offer more value and style to customers. The pioneer store is at corner Speke Avenue and First Street in Harare. Jet stores have spread nationwide and the retail outlet has 28 branches across Zimbabwe to date- from Zvishavane, Gokwe, Chipinge, and Kadoma, to Chinhoyi, Bindura, Gwanda, and other towns. We continue expanding and widening our reach.
